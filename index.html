About the Conference

This regional conference brings together stakeholders from across Sub-Saharan Africa to discuss strategies for transforming apprenticeship systems in the informal economy. It aims to promote youth employability through policy dialogue, shared best practices, and innovative partnerships.

Conference Themes

  • Innovative models of apprenticeship for informal sector skills development
  • Strengthening policy frameworks to support apprenticeships
  • Financing and incentivizing apprenticeship systems
  • Gender and inclusion in informal economy training programs

Programs & Activities

The conference features keynote presentations, panel discussions, workshops, exhibitions, and a youth innovation showcase. Participants will have opportunities to network and explore collaborative projects.

Key Speakers

A distinguished lineup of government officials, experts, and youth leaders from across Africa and beyond will share their experiences and insights on apprenticeship systems and youth employment strategies.
